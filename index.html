The Bass Guitar


What is a bass?

The bass guitar is known for being the lowest-pitched member among its family members. Its appareance is similar to the electric guitar, and has four to six cords. Its popularity has been growing exponencially, you may even know a bass guitar playing without knowing!


There is a whole debate on how to play a bass guitar. Those who use a pick call the finger-style as purist, when those who use finger-style consider themselves as true bassists and think others aren't real bassists.


But the whole debate is purely subjective. There is no correct way to play a bass: any method that makes the bass make a sound is valid.
